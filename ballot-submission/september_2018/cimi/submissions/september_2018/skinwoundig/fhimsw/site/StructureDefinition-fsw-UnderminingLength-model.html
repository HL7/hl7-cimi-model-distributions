UnderminingLengthModel

 

The official URL for this logical model is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/fsw/StructureDefinition/fsw-UnderminingLength-model

Undermining of a wound refers to tissue destruction that extends under the intact wound edge. The wound is larger at its base (wound bed) than at the skin surface. With pressure ulcers, undermining may be caused by a bacterial infection or associated with osteomyelitis. - LOINC 72293-4 (Undermining [Length] of Wound

This logical model was published on Tue Aug 21 00:00:00 EDT 2018 as a draft by HL7 Patient Care and Clinical Information Modeling Initiative Work Groups.

This IG does not define a corresponding profile for this logical model.
