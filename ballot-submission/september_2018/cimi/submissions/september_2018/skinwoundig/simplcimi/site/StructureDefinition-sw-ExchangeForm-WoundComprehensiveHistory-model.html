ExchangeForm-WoundComprehensiveHistoryModel

 

The official URL for this logical model is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/sw/StructureDefinition/sw-ExchangeForm-WoundComprehensiveHistory-model

A collection of all information about a single wound, possibly including many observations taken by different people at different times. Everything in the aggregation relates to the same wound.

This logical model was published on Fri Aug 17 00:00:00 EDT 2018 as a draft by HL7 Patient Care and Clinical Information Modeling Initiative Work Groups.
