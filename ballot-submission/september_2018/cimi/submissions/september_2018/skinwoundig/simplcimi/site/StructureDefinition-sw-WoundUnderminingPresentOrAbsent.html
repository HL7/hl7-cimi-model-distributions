WoundUnderminingPresentOrAbsentProfile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/sw/StructureDefinition/sw-WoundUnderminingPresentOrAbsent

The presence or absence of deep tissue (subcutaneous fat and muscle) damage around the wound margin. Undermining are narrow sinus tracts away from the wound margins and go downward into the wound.

This profile builds on Basic.

This profile was published on Fri Aug 17 00:00:00 EDT 2018 as a draft by HL7 Patient Care and Clinical Information Modeling Initiative Work Groups.
