ComponentOnlyNonLaboratoryObservationModel

 

The official URL for this logical model is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/sw/StructureDefinition/cimi-statement-ComponentOnlyNonLaboratoryObservation-model

An observation with components, but without a result value or panel members.

This logical model was published on Fri Aug 17 00:00:00 EDT 2018 as a draft by HL7 Patient Care and Clinical Information Modeling Initiative Work Groups.
