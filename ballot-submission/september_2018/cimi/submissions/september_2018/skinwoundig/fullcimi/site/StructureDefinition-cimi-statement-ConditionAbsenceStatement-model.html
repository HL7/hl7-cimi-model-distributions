ConditionAbsenceStatementModel

 

The official URL for this logical model is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/sw/StructureDefinition/cimi-statement-ConditionAbsenceStatement-model

A finding that a condition is or was not present in the subject at a certain time, not necessarily the time the information is gathered.

This logical model was published on Fri Aug 17 00:00:00 EDT 2018 as a draft by HL7 Patient Care and Clinical Information Modeling Initiative Work Groups.
