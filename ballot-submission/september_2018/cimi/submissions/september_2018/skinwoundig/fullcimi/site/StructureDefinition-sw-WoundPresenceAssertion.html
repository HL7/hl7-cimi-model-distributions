WoundPresenceAssertionProfile

 

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/sw/StructureDefinition/sw-WoundPresenceAssertion

A determination that a wound that exists at a particular body site. The WoundIdentifier serves as a link between this assertion and assessments of the wound that might occur at later times. Multiple wound assessments can be associated with a single wound.

This profile builds on US Core Condition (a.k.a Problem) Profile.

This profile was published on Fri Aug 17 00:00:00 EDT 2018 as a draft by HL7 Patient Care and Clinical Information Modeling Initiative Work Groups.
