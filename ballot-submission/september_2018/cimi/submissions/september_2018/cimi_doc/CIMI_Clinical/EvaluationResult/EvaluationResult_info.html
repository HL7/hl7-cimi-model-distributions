CIMI_Clinical.EvaluationResult

Overview of the CIMI Clinical Reference Model Module EvaluationResult Package

The EvaluationResult is the second CIMI findings pattern. It is used to capture findings that are expressed in the form of question-anwer pairs such as lab results or the questions and corresponding selected answers collected from a questionnaire. They are typically used to model measurements or the result of laboratory tests.
